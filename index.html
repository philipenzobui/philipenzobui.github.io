About me

I am currently a Master's student at Université de Montréal in Applied Mathematics under the supervision of Professor Maciej Augustyniak. I am broadly interested in the development of novel methodologies to extract meaningful patterns and information from data. This encompasses all ideas from deep learning, time-series forecasting, reinforcement learning, Bayesian machine learning, causal inference, and natural language processing. I am familiar with most fundamental machine learning frameworks and associated Python librairies.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Actuarial Science and worked as an actuary for 2 years. Although I've since transitioned from the field, this experience served as a cornerstone for my proficiency in leveraging probability and statistical methodologies to address complex challenges and applications.
